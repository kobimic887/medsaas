<objective>
Make the four company-branded platform emails (member invite, verification, welcome, password reset) render the sending company's saved brand colour, inlined directly into the email HTML per-send. Companies with no saved palette fall back to DEFAULT_BRAND_PALETTE with no broken or unstyled output, and any palette-resolution error fails open so a send never breaks because branding is missing or malformed.

Purpose: EMAIL-01 (branded emails use the company colour inline) and EMAIL-02 (no-palette fallback to the default brand colour) — the last two open requirements of the v3 Company Brand Colour milestone.

Output: palette-parameterized email templates, the two live sends (invite, reset) wired to resolve and pass a branded palette, and a verification script proving custom-vs-default colour rendering.
</objective>
